Our Mission

At 12th & Pine, our mission is to provide an unparalleled selection of fine wines and spirits that cater to both connoisseurs and casual enthusiasts. We believe in the art of storytelling through every bottle, offering our customers not just products, but experiences that celebrate craftsmanship, heritage, and innovation.

Our History

Founded in 2010, 12th & Pine began as a small boutique store with a vision to bring the finest wines and spirits from renowned vineyards and distilleries to our local community. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings, established partnerships with top producers worldwide, and built a loyal customer base that values quality and authenticity.

Our Team

Our team is composed of passionate individuals who share a deep appreciation for fine wines and spirits. From sommeliers to sales experts, each member brings unique expertise and dedication to ensure that our customers receive personalized recommendations and exceptional service.
